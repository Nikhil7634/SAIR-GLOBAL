BHRINGRAJ

Bhringraj, also known as Eclipta alba, is a medicinal herb widely used in Ayurvedic medicine. It is renowned for its benefits in promoting hair growth, preventing hair loss, and treating scalp issues. Bhringraj is also believed to improve liver function, skin health, and overall wellness.

SHATAVARI

Shatavari, a powerful adaptogenic herb in Ayurvedic medicine, is known for supporting women’s health, particularly reproductive and hormonal balance. Derived from the Asparagus racemosus plant, it enhances fertility, boosts immunity, and aids in digestion.

Spirulina

Spirulina is a blue-green algae that is highly nutritious and often considered a superfood. It is rich in protein, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. TULSI

Tulsi is also known to boost immunity, improve respiratory health, support digestion, and provide antioxidant and anti-inflammatory benefits.
